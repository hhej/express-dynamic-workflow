**Assessment:** ACCEPTABLE. The defensive guard was unreachable in practice anyway — `GUARD_INPUT_USE_LLM_FALLBACK` defaults to `False` so `_llm_fallback` is not invoked for the 4 regression cases (the regex `_classify` catches cases 1 and 3; cases 2 and 4 never reach guard_input's LLM fallback because they route through planner). The layer-differentiation invariant the monkeypatch was meant to protect (category strings distinguishing guard_input vs planner refusals) is still proven by the per-case `guard_decision.category` assertion. The auto-fix is documented transparently in `999.10-03-SUMMARY.md` lines 86-93 as a Rule 1 (Plan-as-Written Wrong) deviation. Test strength of assertion is identical; no scope creep.
