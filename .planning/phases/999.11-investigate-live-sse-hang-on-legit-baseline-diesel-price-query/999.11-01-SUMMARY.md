## Decisions Made

Six implementation decisions documented in frontmatter `key-decisions`. Highlights:

- **180s httpx client timeout** chosen as 3x the observed 60s live hang — captures the full failure surface so we don't lose the post-60s tail of evidence.
- **Readiness poll is separate from elapsed_ms** — uvicorn bind time is excluded from the D-09 30s window so the metric matches how the live demo will be timed (request-to-answer, not boot-to-answer).
- **Probe catches exceptions silently** inside `_drain_sse` and stores `repr(exc)` as `error_str` — the hang itself is the diagnostic; raising would lose evidence we need for Plan 02–04 analysis.
- **Verdict is computed by the probe**, not by the orchestrator. `run_5x.sh` just counts `verdict=PASS_UNDER_30S` records. Keeps the 30s threshold as a single source of truth.
